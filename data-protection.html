How we handle your data

Four commitments, in plain terms

Automatic NDA Protection

Every customer engagement is automatically protected by a mutual non-disclosure agreement. Your inputs, strategies, and competitive intelligence are legally protected from the moment you begin.

Encrypted AWS Data Storage

All data is stored on AWS with enterprise-grade encryption at rest and in transit. We follow AWS security best practices and maintain strict access controls so only you and your authorized team members can access your information.

Your Data Is Never Used to Train AI

Your company's data, conversations, and strategic inputs are never used to train AI models, ours or anyone else's. What you share with Simple Genius stays with Simple Genius.

Enterprise Governance Support

We support vendor security questionnaires, negotiated service agreements, and enterprise data governance requirements. If your organization has specific compliance or security review needs, our team will work through them with you.

The principle behind all of it

Security and ownership are not features. They are the foundation.

Questions about data security?

Our team is happy to walk through any security or compliance questions before you get started.
